# About Page Override — Factual Authority

## Job

Turn the confidence established on Home into trust in the person: education, experience, working philosophy, capabilities, recognition, and operating method.

## Visual treatment

- Use the approved portrait inside the same black operator frame language as Home.
- Keep the hero on the paper canvas; signal red is an accent and CTA, not half the viewport.
- Use an ink field only for the systems philosophy and principles, where authority is useful.
- Use cyan and yellow as section-edge signals for capabilities and recognition, not section backgrounds.
- Preserve generous reading measures and factual chronology.

## Content hierarchy

1. Portrait + engineering thesis.
2. Systems philosophy.
3. Current education/profile facts.
4. Three-role experience timeline.
5. Capability index.
6. Recognition and documented credentials.
7. Operating principles.
8. Project inquiry.

No testimonial, client-logo, or inflated metric substitutes for evidence.
